[viewBag] title = "What is balance for?" ==

Placing ads on the State Commodity and Raw Materials Exchange of Turkmenistan website is a paid service. The cost of one publication is 100 manats.

The funds in the personal account are used only for posting ads on the site. You cannot share funds or transfer them from one account to another, even if both accounts belong to you.

If you have created some ads, but the balance is empty, those ads will be placed in the drafts. You can publish them as soon as there are enough funds in your account.

You can find out more about payment methods in the section "How to top up your account".
